- Reworked the inflight consumed-capacity readout into two centered rows so the label and mAh value no longer overlap in the narrow throttle panel.

- Moved the consumed-capacity readout fully inside the throttle panel and away from the panel border.

- Replaced the stock center header logo with `ETHOS // ROTORFLIGHT`.

- Added a direct telemetry-task fallback.
